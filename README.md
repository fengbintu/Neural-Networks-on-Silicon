# Neural Networks on Silicon

Fengbin Tu is an Assistant Professor and the Associate Director of the Institute of Integrated Circuits and Systems at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, NSFC Excellent Young Scientist, and a core faculty member of the AI Chip Center for Emerging Smart Systems (ACCESS) under InnoHK. For more informantion about Dr. Tu, please refer to [his homepage](https://fengbintu.github.io/). Dr. Tu's main research interest is AI chip and system. This is an exciting field where fresh ideas come out every day, so he's collecting works on related topics. Welcome to join!
